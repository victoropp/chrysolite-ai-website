# Chrysolite AI — ISO 27001:2022 Policy Library

**Organisation:** Chrysolite AI Ltd
**Standard:** ISO/IEC 27001:2022
**Policy Owner:** Chief Information Security Officer (CISO)
**Review Cycle:** Annual (or after any significant change)
**Classification:** Internal — Confidential

---

## Purpose

This folder contains the complete set of organisational policies required to establish, implement, maintain, and continually improve Chrysolite AI's Information Security Management System (ISMS) in accordance with ISO/IEC 27001:2022.

## ISMS Scope

The ISMS covers all information assets, systems, personnel, and processes involved in the development, operation, and delivery of the Chrysolite AI ERP platform, including:

- The chrysoliteai.com marketing and customer portal
- The Chrysolite AI SaaS ERP application and associated APIs
- Internal development, CI/CD, and infrastructure tooling
- Customer data processed and stored on behalf of clients
- All Chrysolite AI employees, contractors, and third-party suppliers with access to company systems

All policies must be reviewed and approved by the CISO and CEO annually. Any material change to scope, technology, or regulatory environment triggers an out-of-cycle review within 30 days.

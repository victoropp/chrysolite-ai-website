## 1. Purpose

This policy establishes the overarching commitment and direction for information security at Chrysolite AI Ltd ("Chrysolite AI" or "the Company"). It provides the foundation for the Information Security Management System (ISMS) in accordance with ISO/IEC 27001:2022.

---

## 2. Scope

This policy applies to:

- All employees, contractors, consultants, interns, and temporary staff
- All information assets owned or processed by Chrysolite AI, including customer data
- All systems, networks, applications, and third-party services used in the delivery of Chrysolite AI products and services
- All physical and virtual locations where Chrysolite AI information is processed or stored

---

## 3. Policy Statement

Chrysolite AI is committed to protecting the confidentiality, integrity, and availability (CIA) of all information it creates, stores, processes, or transmits. Information security is a business priority and a shared responsibility across the entire organisation.

Chrysolite AI shall:

1. **Establish and maintain an ISMS** aligned with ISO/IEC 27001:2022 to systematically manage information security risks
2. **Protect customer data** entrusted to Chrysolite AI, treating it with the highest standards of care
3. **Comply with all applicable laws and regulations**, including GDPR, the UK Data Protection Act 2018, and any sector-specific requirements applicable to customer industries
4. **Continually improve** information security controls and processes based on risk assessments, incidents, audits, and changing threat landscapes
5. **Promote a security-aware culture** through training, communication, and leadership by example
6. **Ensure business continuity** by protecting critical systems and data against disruption, damage, or loss

---

## 4. Information Security Objectives

Chrysolite AI sets the following measurable security objectives, reviewed annually:

| Objective | Metric | Target |
|-----------|--------|--------|
| No critical unpatched vulnerabilities | Days to patch critical CVEs | ≤ 7 days |
| Security awareness training completion | % of staff trained per year | 100% |
| Incident response time | Time to contain a confirmed incident | ≤ 4 hours |
| System availability | Uptime of production services | ≥ 99.9% |
| Access reviews completed | % of access rights reviewed per quarter | 100% |
| Supplier security assessments | % of critical suppliers assessed annually | 100% |

---

## 5. Responsibilities

| Role | Responsibility |
|------|---------------|
| **CEO** | Ultimate accountability for information security; approves this policy |
| **CISO** | Owns the ISMS; develops and maintains policies; reports to CEO |
| **All Employees** | Comply with all information security policies; report incidents promptly |
| **IT / Engineering** | Implement technical security controls; maintain secure systems |
| **Legal / Compliance** | Ensure regulatory compliance; review contracts for security clauses |
| **HR** | Enforce security in onboarding, offboarding, and disciplinary processes |
| **Third-Party Suppliers** | Comply with Chrysolite AI's supplier security requirements |

---

## 6. Consequences of Non-Compliance

Violations of this policy or any subordinate ISMS policies may result in:

- Formal disciplinary action, up to and including termination of employment or contract
- Legal action where applicable
- Notification to relevant regulatory authorities
